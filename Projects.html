Project Description:
 This was my Toyota Highlight project, I created a network of sensors around the plant and used them to reduce TMMTN's compliance risk. There had been explosions caused by excess pressure difference so I created a way to monitor it from your desk and get alerts when the pressure met a certain threshold. This taught me a lot about networking as I had a main server and a lot of sensors communicating to it through 900 MHz radio frequencies.
